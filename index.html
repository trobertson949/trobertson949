Peanut Butter Collective

About

Odell Peanut Butter Collective was inspired by the creative genius of the Erdman common room squad and one hungry artist. After a long night/morning in the painting studio, Danika decided to make the best peanut butter ever. She succeeded. However, she wanted to share her gift without contributing to the capitalist system. Thus, Odell Peanut Butter is a collective, with patrons taking what they want and giving what they are able to. The rest is history.

If you are reading this, Danika is probably making peanut butter RIGHT NOW.
